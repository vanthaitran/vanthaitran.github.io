About

I am a researcher working at the intersection of robotics and additive manufacturing. My interests focus on developing robotic systems, sensing, and control methods that enable precise fabrication, inspection, and repair in real-world environments.

Research Interests

  • Robotic systems for additive manufacturing and in-situ fabrication
  • Legged and mobile robots for operation on unstructured terrain
  • Perception, calibration, and pose estimation for field robotics
  • Motion planning, kinematics, and repeatability analysis
  • Process monitoring and quality evaluation in advanced manufacturing

Detailed project descriptions are shared selectively after publication or public release.
